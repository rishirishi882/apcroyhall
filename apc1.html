A.P.C ROY HALL

ABOUT A.P.C ROY HALL

Acharya Prafulla Chandra Roy Hall is the hostel for the 1st year boys of Kalyani Govt.Engg College.This hostel was established on the 11th day of July of 2003.It is a 4 storied building. Each floor contains 10 rooms with four-five boarders in ecah room.The hostel has its own volleyball ground.Every year the boarders celebrate Saraswati Puja and Hall Day called "XENOPHILIX".The boarders participate in all tournaments held by other hostels.Our superintendent is the Deputy Register of our college D. Asit Baran Kesh.
